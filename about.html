About

One philosophy. Three connected roles.

WE ARE WITH YOU, GYCO, and NADO School are connected parts of one educational ecosystem. Each has a clear role.

The front door

WE ARE WITH YOU

The public-facing platform, where hospitals, schools, senior communities, family organizations, and global partners connect with our work. Visitors first see the people we serve, the programs we create, and the hope we build together.

The student community

GYCO

Where students begin with music and grow through performance, teaching, leadership, service, and collaboration. GYCO prepares students to bring their talents into the world with purpose.

The learning system

NADO School

The educational philosophy behind student growth. It helps students understand that everyone can learn, everyone can teach, and everyone has something to share. It turns experience into education.

The ecosystem

How everything connects

NADO School teaches the philosophy. GYCO forms the student community. WE ARE WITH YOU brings that learning into real communities.

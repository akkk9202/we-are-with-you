# Partner Outreach Email Template

> **Purpose:** A warm, humble first-contact email to a prospective partner — a hospital, senior community, school, or community organization.

## How to use this template

1. Copy this file, fill in every `______` blank, delete unused guidance.
2. Keep it short (a screen or less). Warm, student-led, no hype.
3. **This audience may be vulnerable — never overpromise.** Offer presence and encouragement, not outcomes or medical benefit.
4. **Sending requires human approval.** Do not send automatically. Log the send in your outreach tracker.

## Tone rules

- [ ] Humble and student-led — "we are a group of students who…"
- [ ] No promises about results, healing, or specific outcomes
- [ ] Respectful of the partner's time and their community's privacy
- [ ] Consistent with the tagline: *"Even Here. Even Now. We Are With You."*
- [ ] Reviewed against `context/brand_voice.md`

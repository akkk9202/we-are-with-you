# Follow-up Email Template

> **Purpose:** Polite follow-ups after no reply, after a first call, or when sharing materials. Pick one variant.

## How to use this template

1. Choose the variant that fits the situation.
2. Fill in every `______` blank; delete the other variants.
3. Same tone rules as outreach — warm, humble, never pushy, never overpromising to vulnerable audiences.
4. **Sending requires human approval.** Log the send in your outreach tracker.

## Tone rules

- [ ] Gentle, patient, no pressure
- [ ] Grateful, not transactional
- [ ] No outcome/medical promises
- [ ] Reviewed against `context/brand_voice.md`

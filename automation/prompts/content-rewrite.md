Pair with `../../skills/content_rewrite_for_website.md` and `../../context/brand_voice.md`.

> Rewrite the following copy for the WE ARE WITH YOU site. The audience includes patients, families

>
> Rules:
> - Warm, plain, calm, and honest. No hype, no urgency, no marketing gloss.

> - Keep the tagline wording exact: "Even Here. Even Now. We Are With You."
> - Preserve any names, Korean text, and links exactly.
> - Match the length of the original unless asked to change it.
>
> Return only the rewritten copy. Then list, in one line each, what you changed and why.

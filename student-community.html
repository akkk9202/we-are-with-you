More than an orchestra

GYCO is the student community behind WE ARE WITH YOU. Students begin with music, but they grow through teaching, leadership, service, research, media, and real community experiences. GYCO is where students learn together — WE ARE WITH YOU is where they bring that learning into the world.

What is GYCO?

A student-centered orchestra and educational community

GYCO — the Greater Youth Collaborative Opus — is where young musicians learn, perform, teach, serve, and lead. Students develop musical skills through rehearsals, performances, and collaboration, but the purpose of GYCO reaches beyond the stage.

Through community partnerships and student-led projects, members learn how to use their abilities to create meaningful connection.

What students do

Learn on stage. Lead off stage.

  • Orchestra rehearsals
  • Community performances
  • Senior living outreach
  • Healthcare and family support programs
  • Music teaching
  • Teaching videos
  • Song request projects
  • Research and reflection
  • Media and website development
  • Leadership teams
  • Educational resource creation
  • Community partnerships
How GYCO connects

Students learn in GYCO — and bring what they learn into WE ARE WITH YOU

GYCO gives students a place to learn. WE ARE WITH YOU gives students a place to serve.

Students first develop discipline, teamwork, and musical ability through GYCO. Then they use those skills to create partner pages, teaching videos, music experiences, song requests, letters, activities, and community projects through WE ARE WITH YOU.

Begin with music. Grow through service.

GYCO isn't only about becoming a better musician.

It's about becoming a person who can listen, lead, teach, serve, and create meaningful impact.

Taps of
Love

Sometimes hope arrives in only a few words.

Short messages of encouragement created to remind patients, families, caregivers, healthcare professionals, and community members that they are not alone.

♡
The Program

What Is Taps of Love?

Taps of Love is a growing collection of short video and written messages from students, healthcare professionals, volunteers, families, and community members.

Each message is simple and personal.

A participant may share gratitude, encouragement, hope, a meaningful reflection, or a reminder to keep going.

These messages can be viewed through digital platforms, QR codes, brochures, community displays, and partner organizations.

The Impact

Why Short Messages Matter

People going through difficult experiences may not always have the energy or time to watch a long performance or attend an event.

A short message can still create a meaningful moment.

  • →

    It can remind a patient that someone is thinking about them.

  • →

    It can thank a caregiver whose work often goes unnoticed.

  • →

    It can encourage a family during a long hospital stay.

  • →

    It can allow a healthcare professional to speak beyond a clinical interaction and offer genuine human support.

A few words may not change the entire journey, but they can change one moment within it.

Participation

Who Can Share a Message?

Participants do not need professional recording equipment or public-speaking experience. Messages should feel sincere, respectful, and personal.

🎓

Students

Share encouragement, reflections, and hope from your own learning journey.

🏥

Healthcare Professionals

Offer words that go beyond the clinical interaction.

🤝

Caregivers

Share what you've learned from walking alongside someone through difficulty.

💛

Patients & Survivors

Your experience is a message of courage and hope in itself.

✋

Volunteers

Share your reason for showing up and what it means to you.

🌍

Community Members

Anyone with a word of encouragement, gratitude, or hope.

Message Ideas

Suggested Message Prompts

❝

What would you say to someone facing a difficult day?

❝

What gives you hope?

❝

What would you like to thank a caregiver or healthcare worker for?

❝

What is one thing you want a patient or family to remember?

❝

Who encouraged you during a difficult time?

❝

How can we remind someone that they are not alone?

The Process

How It Works

Step 1

Choose a Message Prompt

Select from the suggested prompts or write your own sincere response.

Step 2

Record or Write

Create a short video or written message. Simple and sincere is all that's needed.

Step 3

Submit for Review

Submit your message for review and approval to ensure it meets community guidelines.

Step 4

Share

Approved messages are organized and shared through digital platforms, QR codes, and partner organizations.

Step 5

Pass It Forward

Viewers may be invited to create messages for someone else — continuing the cycle.

Safety & Respect

Safety and Respect

All content should protect the privacy, dignity, and well-being of participants.

Messages should not contain private medical details, medical advice, promises about recovery, or information that identifies patients without permission.

Healthcare organizations and community partners may require additional review, consent, branding, or participation guidelines.

Learning

Everyone can learn. Everyone can teach. Everyone has something to share.

NADO School is the learning system behind our student growth. It is built on the belief that education does not only happen in classrooms — learning can happen through music, service, family, research, community, storytelling, teaching, and real-life experience.

What is NADO School?

Turning experience into growth

NADO School is the educational foundation that helps students turn experience into growth. Students learn by doing. They learn by teaching. They learn by serving. They learn by reflecting. They learn by creating something useful for someone else.

The word NADO means "me too" in Korean — the belief that every person can join the learning process.

나도
Our learning philosophy

Learn. Grow. Serve. Share.

Students learn a skill or idea.
They grow through practice and reflection.
They serve by using what they learn for others.
They share by creating resources, lessons, videos, books, or programs another person can use.

This cycle connects NADO School, GYCO, and WE ARE WITH YOU.

Learning connects to service

Learning is not complete until it is shared

A student who learns music can teach rhythm. A student who writes a workbook can help another student study. A student who records a video can reach someone far away. A student who reflects on a community project can improve the next one.

NADO School helps students understand that learning is not only for personal achievement — it is preparation for service.

Learning becomes stronger when it is shared.

NADO School helps students grow. GYCO gives students a community. WE ARE WITH YOU gives students a way to serve.

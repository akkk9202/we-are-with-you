What is NADO School?

A School Beyond Walls

A school is more than a building. It exists wherever people learn from one another and share what they have learned. At NADO School, everyone can be both a learner and a teacher.

Learning becomes sharing. Sharing inspires new learning. New learning becomes growth.

나도

"Nado" · Korean · "me too."

What does "NADO" mean?

More than agreement.
A personal choice to join.

"Nado" (나도) is a Korean word that means "me too." At NADO School, it means more than agreement — it is the moment someone chooses to participate. A choice to say:

"I will learn, too."

"I will create, too."

"I will share, too."

"I will grow, too."

Every journey begins with one person's decision. That decision is NADO.

A choice to join

One person says "Nado."
Then that person becomes part of "we."

To learn. To create. To share. To grow.

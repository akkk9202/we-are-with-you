A WE ARE WITH YOU program

Hope Capsule

A small collection of messages, music, stories, and reflections created for moments when someone needs hope.

Some words of encouragement deserve to be revisited. A Hope Capsule gathers messages, songs, photographs, interviews, and stories into a digital collection that can be opened again and again in a hospital room, a family support house, a classroom, or a quiet moment at home.

Each capsule preserves and shares stories of hope with communities around the world.

How capsules are created

Stories Gathered by Students

Hope Capsules are created by GYCO Student Correspondents, students who document, preserve, and share stories from GYCO, NADO School, WE ARE WITH YOU, and partner communities.

Listen

Stories are gathered

With permission, student correspondents interview patients, families, older adults, volunteers, educators, and community leaders while documenting performances, projects, and service activities.

Create

Stories become media

Students transform interviews, articles, photographs, videos, letters, and reflections into digital media while developing practical skills in writing, photography, and storytelling.

Share

Media becomes a capsule

Each collection becomes a digital Hope Capsule that can be opened through partner pages and QR codes and exchanged with student correspondents in other countries.

Where capsules live

Every Partner Page Includes One

Every WE ARE WITH YOU partner page includes a Hope Capsule created for that community: quiet and gentle for the NICU, cheerful for family support houses, and rich with memories for senior communities.

As the correspondent network grows, students from South Korea, the Philippines, Malawi, and future partner countries will exchange capsules and encourage one another through a global community of learning and service.

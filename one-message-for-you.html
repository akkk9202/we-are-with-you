Every act of encouragement begins with one simple message.

One Message for You invites people to share words of hope, gratitude, kindness, and compassion with someone who may be facing illness, disability, loneliness, or a difficult season of life.

Together, these individual messages become part of something much larger — a growing community of encouragement through WE ARE WITH YOU.

Where your message goes

The first step of every partnership

Whether we work with a hospital, a Ronald McDonald House, a school, or a senior community, every partnership begins with One Message for You. Messages reach patients, families, caregivers, seniors, students, and staff through partner pages, QR codes, printed materials, and events.

Students then transform these messages into music, digital stories, and meaningful experiences — so one message keeps traveling long after it is written.

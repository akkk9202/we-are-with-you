The Initiative

WE ARE
WITH YOU

Even Here. Even Now.

A community initiative that transforms music from something people simply receive into something they can learn, experience, shape, and share.

Why We Exist

No One Should Walk Through a Difficult Experience Alone

People facing illness, isolation, uncertainty, or major life changes often need more than entertainment.

They need to feel recognized.

They need opportunities to express themselves.

They need moments where they can participate rather than simply observe.

They need reminders that their stories, preferences, memories, and voices still matter.

WE ARE WITH YOU creates those moments through music, teaching, conversation, participation, and reflection.

What Makes It Different

Not Only For You

Traditional outreach often involves a group preparing something and presenting it to an audience.

That can still be meaningful, but the relationship often ends when the performance ends.

Created With You

WE ARE WITH YOU invites participants into the experience.

They may request a song, sing with musicians, learn an instrument, watch a teaching video, share a memory, provide feedback, or help shape a future program.

The community is not simply receiving the project. The community becomes part of the project.

Our Framework

How the Circle of Love Works

Step 1

A Student Shares

A GYCO student performs, teaches, records a lesson, listens, or creates an opportunity for participation.

Step 2

A Participant Responds

A patient, family member, healthcare professional, senior, or community member joins the activity.

Step 3

An Experience Is Created

The participants sing, play, learn, request, reflect, or share together.

Step 4

The Experience Is Remembered

Participants may share feedback, stories, photographs, messages, or meaningful moments from the activity.

Step 5

The Experience Continues

What was learned becomes part of the next performance, lesson, teaching video, or community project.

The Circle of Love turns outreach into an ongoing relationship.

Inclusion

Who Can Participate?

WE ARE WITH YOU is designed for people of different ages, backgrounds, abilities, and musical experiences. Previous musical experience is not required. Participation begins with curiosity and a willingness to connect.
